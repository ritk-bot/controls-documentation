
## Start of architecture research 

We shall be trying to make our primary motherboard mcu, the STM32 F411CEU6 communicate with our daughter board mcu, the ESP32. We shall be trying three main formats:
- SPI
- UART
- CAN

First I started off by making my STM32 ready by soldering on the header pins.

After that I downloaded the software I needed to code on the board and flash it.

I would suggest going through the datasheet for the board and understanding the things that it has since it is good to know the hardware that you have and what modules the board has like dma controller, peripherals etc.

### Docs for the STM32F411CEU6
- Pinout : https://deepbluembedded.com/stm32f411-black-pill-board-pinout-schematic-datasheet/
- Product Page : https://www.st.com/en/microcontrollers-microprocessors/stm32f411ce.html
- Datasheet : https://www.st.com/resource/en/datasheet/stm32f411ce.pdf
